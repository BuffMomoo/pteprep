PTE in Nepal
Test centres
Nepal has several Pearson-authorised PTE Academic test centres, including in Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan and Biratnagar. Availability changes often, so use the official site to see current dates and locations near you.
Before you book
PTE Academic takes about two hours and covers speaking, writing, reading and listening in one sitting. Most test takers receive results within a few business days through their Pearson account.
